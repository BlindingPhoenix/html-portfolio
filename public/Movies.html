The Best Movies According to Jessica

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Princess Bride

I love this movie because it was the first comedic romance I had watched as a kid and I had my first crush on a grown movie character, Wesley.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Beautiful imagery and it really makes one feel like it's possible to live in that world.

The Game

This movie was the first suspense brain teaser that I had ever seen and I loved that I couldn't guess what the end was going to be
